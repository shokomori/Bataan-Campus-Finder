About Our Project

The Higher Education Navigator Website is a student-developed project created by Grade 12 STEM students of Saint Catherine of Siena Academy of Samal, Inc. This website aims to provide accessible, accurate, and up-to-date information about Higher Education Institutions (HEIs) in the Philippines.

Through this platform, senior high school students can explore various colleges and universities, view their programs, admission requirements, scholarships, tuition fees, and contact information—all in one place.

Our Mission

Our goal is to help students make informed decisions about their educational future and lessen the challenges of searching for reliable information online. We believe that access to quality education starts with access to quality information.
