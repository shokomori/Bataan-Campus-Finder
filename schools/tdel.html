Tomas Del Rosario College

PACU-COA Level II Accredited Private School

Description: Established in 1950, Tomas del Rosario College is a private, non-sectarian educational institution in Balanga, Bataan. As the only PACU-COA Level II accredited private school in Bataan, TRC offers various programs in nursing, accounting, management, marketing, banking, education, and computer science. The institution prides itself on molding students academically, morally, and socially, preparing them for service in business, profession, and government with emphasis on truth and goodness.

Mission: Tomas Del Rosario College aims to produce God-fearing and well-rounded learners who are academically and properly prepared to take higher level of education.

Vision: Truth and goodness in man and for all others through education.
