Philippine Women's University CDCEC Bataan

Career Development & Continuing Education Center

Description: PWU-CDCEC Bataan is a non-stock, non-profit, non-sectarian educational institution affiliated with PWU-Taft, Manila. Established to meet the growing demands for quality continuing education in the global community, it carries the legacy of its mother school founded in 1919.

Mission: To provide quality and accessible education particularly for women, fostering academic excellence, character formation, and social responsibility.

Vision: The institution envisions empowered, competent, and morally grounded women who contribute meaningfully to society.
