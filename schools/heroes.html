Bataan Heroes Memorial College

Established 1979 | Private, Non-Sectarian Institution

Description: Bataan Heroes Memorial College, established in 1979, is a private, non-sectarian, co-educational institution, duly registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) as a stock corporation. BHMC was intended by its founders to serve as a monument to the heroes of Bataan in World War II who left behind legacies of courage, human endurance, burning patriotism, and the indomitability of the Filipino spirit. These are legacies that the founders deemed worthy of emulation and inculcation in the minds of the Filipino youth.

Mission: The college aims to provide holistic education that develops intellectual competence, character formation, and social awareness through quality instruction and community involvement.

Vision: Heroes Memorial College envisions itself as a center of academic excellence committed to producing morally upright, globally competitive, and socially responsible graduates.

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS
Open Admission Policy: Bataan Heroes Memorial College welcomes all students with an inclusive admission approach. No entrance exam, no grade requirements, no bridging program, no physical requirements (such as height), and no age requirements. Any Senior High School strand or track is accepted in any college program.

Required Documents:

Copy of PSA Birth Certificate
Original Grade 12 Report Card
Original Certificate of Good Moral Character
5 pieces 2x2 ID pictures
2 Long Brown Envelopes
Application Process: For more information about the admission process and application procedures, contact the Admissions Office at information@heroes1979.edu.ph or call 0931 016 3051.

SCHOLARSHIPS & FINANCIAL AID

Bataan Heroes Memorial College offers comprehensive scholarship programs to support deserving students throughout their academic journey. From entrance scholarships to awards for outstanding academic performance and leadership, BHMC is committed to making quality education accessible.

Academic Scholarships

Academic Merit Scholarship

With Highest Honors (Grade 12 graduation): 100% tuition fee discount for the 1st semester of 1st year

With High Honors (Grade 12 graduation): 50% tuition fee discount for the 1st semester of 1st year

How to Apply: Through the Admissions Office

Laureana S. Rosales Scholarship for Outstanding Students

Coverage: 100% tuition and miscellaneous fee discount

Eligibility: Students who top the scholarship exam

Open to: Incoming freshmen

Requirements: Maintaining GWA of 2.0 with no failing grade

Renewable: Every semester

How to Apply: Through the Admissions Office

Sesenio S. Rosales Scholarship for Civil Engineering Students

Coverage: 100% tuition and miscellaneous fee

Eligibility: CE students who top the scholarship exam

Open to: Incoming freshmen

Requirements: Must have no failing grade

Renewable: Every semester

How to Apply: Through the Admissions Office

Entrance Scholarship
