ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

To apply for admission at College of Subic Montessori Inc., please prepare and submit the following documents:

Form 138 or Report Card (Original)
Form 137 or Transcript of Record (Original)
Good Moral Certificate (Original)
Diploma (Photocopy)
PSA Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
2 pieces of 2x2 Picture (Recent, white background)
2 pieces of 1x1 Picture (Recent, white background)
3 pieces of Long Brown Envelope
3 pieces of Plastic Envelope

Important Notes: All original documents will be verified and returned after enrollment. Photocopies must be clear and legible. Additional requirements may be requested depending on your chosen program or student classification (freshman, transferee, etc.).

APPLICATION PROCESS

Follow these steps to complete your application at College of Subic Montessori Inc.:

1. Visit the Campus

Go to the College of Subic Montessori Inc. campus at Sta. Isabel, Dinalupihan, Bataan. Visit the Registrar's Office during office hours to inquire about admission procedures.

2. Get Application Form

Request and fill out the application form from the Registrar's Office. Provide accurate and complete information. You may also inquire via email or phone before visiting.

3. Submit Requirements

Submit your completed application form along with all required documents. Make sure all original documents are ready for verification and photocopies are properly prepared.

4. Take Entrance Exam

Schedule and take the entrance examination. The exam schedule will be provided by the Admissions Office. Bring a valid ID and writing materials on exam day.

5. Wait for Admission Results

Wait for the results of your entrance examination. You will be notified about your admission status. Qualified students will receive instructions for the next steps.

6. Enroll

Once accepted, proceed with enrollment. Pay the required fees and complete your registration during the enrollment period. Attend orientation and receive your class schedule.
