ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

Documentary Requirements:

Recent colored picture
Copy of Birth Certificate
Copy of Diploma, if graduate from Senior High School, or College
Copy of Certification of Registration or Registration Form, if College Level
Copy of Certificate of Enrollment that you are currently enrolled in Grade 12, if Senior High School graduating student
Copy of School ID
Copy of latest School Report Card with at least has a GPA of 83%
Payment of non-refundable processing fee of Php 400.00 in cash on the day of the examination

Qualification Requirements:

Male or Female, Single
Natural Born Filipino
Physically fit
Morally upright and has no derogatory record
Born from 01-January-2003 to 31-December-2008
College Level/Graduate OR Senior High School Graduate/Graduating Student
Minimum height of 5 ft. 4 in. (162.5 cm) for MALE
Minimum height of 5 ft. 2 in. (157.5 cm) for FEMALE
Has a GPA of at least 83%
Has never been officially admitted at MAAP
Free College Entrance Examination (RA 12006):

To avail of the Free Examination Act, students must:

  • Be a natural-born Filipino citizen
  • Belong to the top 10% of their graduating class
  • Come from a family with household income below the poverty threshold (as certified by DSWD and defined by NEDA)

Required Documents:

  • Certificate from Senior High School confirming top 10% class ranking
  • Certified DSWD certificate stating the family income is below the poverty threshold

Note: Failure to submit the required documents during admission will result in disqualification from the program.

Important Reminder: The National Merchant Marine Aptitude Test (NaMMAT) is not required during the MAAP Entrance Examination, which is the first phase of the admission process. However, applicants may be required to submit their NaMMAT score during the Interview Proper, the second phase of the evaluation process. For information on NaMMAT application procedures and requirements, please visit the CHED website at ched.gov.ph
ADMISSION PROCEDURE

Online Application Process:

Step 1: Online Application Form

Visit the Online MAAP Midshipman Application Form at: app.maap.edu.ph:1234/omas_v2/applicantreference.php

Guidelines for Uploading Documents:
  • Files should be in JPG, JPEG or PNG format
  • Each file should not exceed 500KB in size
  • Image size should not exceed 1024 pixels in height and width
  • Ensure that the wordings in your uploaded files are readable
  • If your file is not available, upload an image saying "not available"
  • If the document is not applicable, upload an image saying "not applicable"
  • Do not use special characters in filenames (except for email address)

Application Steps:

Upload your picture and application requirements online
Receive notification of your application status through your email
Receive a MAAP eTestPermit through email if qualified for the entrance examination
Print your eTestPermit and present it on the day of the examination
