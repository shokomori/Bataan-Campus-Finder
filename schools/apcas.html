APPLICATION PROCESS

Ready to start your journey with APCAS? Follow these simple steps to apply:

1. Fill Out Application Form

Complete the online application form by scanning the QR code or visiting this link.

2. Prepare Requirements

Gather all necessary documents including your PSA Birth Certificate, Report Card, and Good Moral Certificate. Ensure all documents are original and complete.

3. Submit Application

Submit your completed application form along with all required documents to the APCAS Admissions Office. You may also email scanned copies to admission@apcas.ph.education.

4. Entrance Examination

Take the APCAS entrance examination. The schedule and venue will be announced by the admissions office. Make sure to bring a valid ID and writing materials.

5. Wait for Results

Examination results will be posted on the APCAS website and social media pages. You may also inquire directly at the admissions office or via email.

6. Enrollment

Once accepted, proceed with the enrollment process. Pay the necessary fees and complete your registration during the scheduled enrollment period.

Need Assistance? For any questions or concerns about the application process, feel free to contact us at admission@apcas.ph.education or call us at (047) 237-4438.
