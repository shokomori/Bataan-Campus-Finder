SCHOLARSHIPS

üéì Republic Act 10931 - Universal Access to Quality Tertiary Education

Coverage: FREE TUITION for all undergraduate students in State Universities and Colleges (SUCs)

Eligibility: All qualified Filipino students enrolled in undergraduate programs at PUP Bataan

Benefits:

  • Free tuition fees
  • Access to quality tertiary education
  • Equal opportunities for higher education
  • Support for Skolars ng Bayan

About the Law: Republic Act 10931, also known as the Universal Access to Quality Tertiary Education Act, was enacted to provide free tuition and other school fees in State Universities and Colleges (SUCs), Local Universities and Colleges (LUCs), and State-run Technical-Vocational Institutions (TVIs). This landmark legislation ensures that no Filipino student is deprived of quality tertiary education due to financial constraints.

Additional Financial Assistance: PUP Bataan may also offer other scholarship programs and financial assistance opportunities. For the most current information about available scholarships and grants, please contact the Office of Student Services or visit the official PUP website at www.pup.edu.ph
