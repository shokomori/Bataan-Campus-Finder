Main Campus

Mission: To develop native leaders and empowered communities by delivering transformative instruction, research and production through Change Drivers and responsive policies.

Vision: An inclusive and sustainable university recognized for its global academic excellence by 2030.

ACADEMIC PROGRAMS

Bataan Peninsula State University Main Campus is well-known in the field of engineering and technology. Since its inception in 1953 (formerly Bataan National School of Arts and Trades or BNSAT), the Main Campus has been among the country's top-producers of high-caliber technician-leaders needed by industries both locally and abroad.
