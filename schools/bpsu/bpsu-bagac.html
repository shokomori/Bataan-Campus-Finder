OVERVIEW

The Bagac Campus serves as a satellite facility for the Bagac community and nearby municipalities. It offers selected undergraduate programs with emphasis on applied sciences, vocational training, and livelihood development to meet local economic needs.

Located in Bagumbayan, Bagac, the campus maintains strong ties with the local government and community organizations to provide relevant education and extension services.
