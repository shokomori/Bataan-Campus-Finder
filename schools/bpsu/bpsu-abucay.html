OVERVIEW

The Abucay Campus of BPSU serves as an extension facility for students in the northern municipalities. It offers undergraduate programs in selected disciplines and maintains strong community partnerships for extension activities and services.

Located in Bangkal, Abucay, the campus is accessible to students from surrounding areas and provides quality education at an affordable cost through the government's free tuition program.
