TUITION & FEES

Republic Act No. 10931 - Free Tertiary Education

Bataan Peninsula State University, as a State University, provides free tuition and school fees under Republic Act No. 10931 (Universal Access to Quality Tertiary Education Act of 2017). This landmark legislation ensures that qualified Filipino students can access higher education without the burden of tuition fees.

Key Features of RA 10931:

Free Higher Education: Undergraduate students in public higher education institutions receive free tuition and school fees
Free Tech-Voc Education: Students in state-run technical-vocational institutions qualify for free programs
Tertiary Education Subsidy (TES): Financial assistance program to support eligible students
Student Loan Program: Available for students needing additional educational financing

Exceptions to Free Tuition:

Students who already have a bachelor's degree
Students who fail to meet admission and retention policies
Students who fail to complete their degree within the prescribed period
Miscellaneous and Other Fees: While tuition is free, students are required to pay miscellaneous fees and other charges such as laboratory fees, ID registration, and development fees. For specific amounts, please contact the Main Campus administration.

APPLICATION PROCESS

1. Online Registration

Visit the BPSU official website and fill out the online application form. Create your student account and provide accurate personal information.

2. Submit Requirements

Upload scanned copies of all required documents. Ensure all files are clear and readable. Original documents will be verified upon enrollment.

3. Entrance Examination

Take the BPSU College Admission Test (BPSU-CAT). Schedule and venue will be announced through email and the university website.

4. Wait for Results

Examination results will be released within 2-3 weeks. Qualified applicants will receive an email notification with further instructions.

5. Enrollment

Once accepted, proceed to the enrollment process. Submit original documents, pay necessary fees, and complete registration within the enrollment period.
