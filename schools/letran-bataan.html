Colegio De San Juan De Letran Bataan

Catholic & Dominican Institution

Mission: A Catholic and Dominican institution with a special devotion to Mary commits itself to evangelization through education towards the integral formation of the human person, in the noble tradition of excellence, according to the ideals of DEUS, PATRIA, LETRAN.

Vision: By 2031, we envision Colegio de San Juan de Letran-Bataan as a leading educational institution in Region III, whose academic programs are distinctively marked with a strong commitment to innovation, collaboration, and sustainability.

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

For New Students:

One (1) 2" x 2" recent picture with white background
Original PSA Birth Certificate
Original Good Moral Character
Photocopy of Baptismal Certificate (for Catholic applicants only)
Original copy of Report Card (SF9) with Principal's signature
For Non-Formal Education Accreditation and Equivalency:
  • Certificate of Rating (with passing marks in all subjects)
  • Learner's Permanent Record (AF-5) with eligibility to college or SHS

For Transferees:

One (1) 2" x 2" recent picture with white background
Original Transcript of Records (TOR) for Collegiate
Original SF 10 (Form 137) for Basic Education
Honorable Dismissal
Original Good Moral Character
Original PSA Birth Certificate
Photocopy of Baptismal Certificate (for Catholic applicants only)

For Foreign Nationals (New Students and Transferees):

Student Study Permit / Student Visa
Scholastic Records, duly authenticated by the Philippine Foreign Service Post located in the student applicant's country of origin or legal residence
Certificate of Local Residency
Alien Certificate of Registration or Special Study Permit
Photocopy of applicant's passport bio-page
English Proficiency Course or submit IELTS, TOEFL, or TESOL certification
Application Process: For more information about the admission process and application procedures, visit the official admission page at letranbataan.edu.ph/Home/Admission
