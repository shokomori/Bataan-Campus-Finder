Limay Polytechnic College

Private Municipal College

Description: Limay Polytechnic College is a private municipal college in Limay, Bataan. It originated from a local-government outreach program under the polytechnic outreach of a public university, with the aim to serve indigent students needing access to higher education. The school emphasizes not only academic competence but also values like service, integrity, and community responsiveness ‚Äî preparing graduates to contribute to development in Limay, Bataan, and beyond.

Vision: Limay Polytechnic College envisions a technically skilled, globally competitive, and values-driven workforce contributing to national development.

ACADEMIC PROGRAMS

Limay Polytechnic College offers a diverse range of programs across education, business, accountancy, and technology. The college has expanded its offerings from secretarial courses to full degree programs, preparing students with both technical skills and strong values for global competitiveness.

TUITION & FEES
Free Higher Education: Limay Polytechnic College is a grantee of Free Higher Education under the Universal Access to Quality Tertiary Education Act (UAQTEA) or RA 10931. Qualified students can benefit from tuition-free education under this program.
